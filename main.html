Discussing the
elephant in the blender

Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world. Indeed, it is the only thing that ever has.

Well, we're three brothers, Ashkan, Arman, and Kian, who envisioned Devlumination. It emerged from our collective desire to create a unified platform that simplifies the job hunting process for fresh software developers and college grads. We're not about idleness; we're about empowerment. Devlumination is the culmination of countless discussions, brainstorming sessions, and engaging with job seekers like you.

We aimed for an intuitive platform that provides valuable resources, insights, and networking opportunities to guide your career journey. Devlumination combines networking, up-to-date news, job hunting strategies, career roadmaps, and more. What you witness is just the beginning.

We call it a blend, for Devlumination aspires to encompass everything we craved during our job hunting experience, helping you illuminate your path to success.
